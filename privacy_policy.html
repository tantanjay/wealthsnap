Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 2026

App Name: WealthSnap

This Privacy Policy explains how WealthSnap ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Overview

WealthSnap is designed as a privacy-first, local-only application. We do not maintain external servers to store your personal account data. Your financial data, transaction logs, and personal details remain stored locally on your device.

2. Information We Collect & How We Use It

2.1 Camera and Images

What we access: We request permission to access your device's camera and photo library.

Why: To allow you to take photos of receipts, invoices, or financial documents for analysis.

Data Handling: Images you select are processed temporarily to extract insights (e.g., transaction details). These images are sent securely to Google's Gemini API for analysis and are not permanently stored on our servers.

2.2 Biometric Data

What we access: We use your device's biometric capabilities (FaceID, TouchID, or Fingerprint).

Why: To provide secure, quick access to your financial data.

Data Handling: We never access, collect, or store your actual biometric data (fingerprints or facial maps). Authentication is handled entirely by your device's operating system, which simply tells our app "Success" or "Failure".

2.3 User Content (Financial Data)

What we access: Information you input, such as transaction history, budgets, and goals.

Data Handling: All such data is stored locally on your device using secure database technologies (SQLite and SecureStore). We do not have access to this data.

3. Third-Party Services

We use specific third-party services to provide app functionality. These services have their own privacy policies:

  • Google Gemini API: Used for AI analysis of images and text. Google Privacy Policy
  • Expo: The platform used to build and update the app. Expo Privacy Policy

4. Data Retention

Since data is stored locally on your device, you have full control. Uninstalling the application will remove all local data associated with it, unless you have created a manual backup file.

5. Children’s Privacy

Our Services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. In the case we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we immediately delete this from our records. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so that we will be able to do necessary actions.
