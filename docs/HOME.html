🔐 Privacy & Security

WealthSnap is built with a zero-knowledge, offline-first philosophy. Even the developer cannot see your data.

AES-256 Encryption

Sensitive data is encrypted locally. No forced cloud sync or remote tracking.

Local Storage Only

Your data lives in a local SQLite database. You control the files and backups.

Secure App Lock

PIN protection and conditional screenshot blocking keep your visibility private.

🤖 AI-Powered Tools (Optional)

  • Smart Receipt Analysis: Automatically extract amount, category, and notes from images.
  • Market Intelligence: AI-assisted stock price retrieval and dividend history research.
  • Smart Advisor: Anomaly detection and spending spike insights derived locally.
  • BYOK (Bring Your Own Key): Full control via your personal Gemini API key.

AI features are strictly opt-in. Images and local data are never used for training or shared permanently.
