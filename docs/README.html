WealthSnap — Your Financial Mirror

An offline-first financial mirror built for absolute privacy. High‑resolution insight into your money — securely stored on your device, exactly where it belongs.

Version: v1.8.0 Last Updated: February 3, 2026 Status: Closed Testing

🔭 Vision & Philosophy

Your Standing, Reflected

WealthSnap is a reflection tool — not a financial advisor. It offers a precise, unbiased view of your financial reality without recommendations or nudges.

Privacy as a Foundation

We don’t just protect your data — we don’t collect it. Your entire financial life stays on your device, not on a server.

Accuracy Above All

Every cent matters. An arbitrary‑precision math engine ensures totals reconcile perfectly, even across years of history.

🌍 What WealthSnap Does

  • Track Everything: Income, expenses, transfers, and assets in one unified view.
  • Investment Mirror: Monitor holdings, cost basis, and allocation without linking a brokerage.
  • Smart Calendar: Visualize upcoming bills and see your real safe‑to‑spend balance.
  • Local Insights: Savings rate, burn rate, and financial runway — derived only from your data.

🔐 Privacy & Security

Even the developer cannot see your data — because it never leaves your device.

100% Offline‑First

No servers, no accounts, no third‑party tracking. The app works fully offline.

Local Encryption

Sensitive fields are encrypted using AES‑256, leveraging device security where supported.

Total Ownership

Your data lives in a local SQLite database. You control the files, backups, and lifecycle.

🤖 Smart Tools (Optional)

  • Receipt Scanning: Extract totals and dates using AI when enabled. Receipt data is stored locally and encrypted.
  • Asset Price Fetching: AI-assisted retrieval and cross-checking of publicly available market prices when enabled.
  • Bring Your Own Key (BYOK): Supply your own Google Gemini API key. Billing and usage are handled directly by the provider.

All smart features are strictly optional and can be disabled at any time.

💡 Why WealthSnap Is Free

No Servers

No infrastructure means no ongoing costs.

No Data Collection

There is nothing to sell, analyze, or monetize.

Built for the Craft

This app exists because the developer needed it — and chose to share it.
