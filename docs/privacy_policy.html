1. Overview

WealthSnap is designed as a privacy-first, local-only application. We do not maintain external servers to store your personal account data. Your financial data, transaction logs, and personal details remain stored locally on your device.

2. Information We Collect & How We Use It

2.1 Camera and Images

What we access: We request permission to access your device's camera and photo library.

Why: To allow you to take photos of receipts, invoices, or financial documents for analysis.

Data Handling: Images you select are processed temporarily to extract insights (e.g., transaction details). These images are sent securely to Google's Gemini API for analysis and are not permanently stored on your device.

API Processing: When you use AI features, image data is sent to Google's Gemini API, which is subject to Google's Privacy Policy. WealthSnap acts merely as a conduit for this data and does not store, process, or have access to the API's internal processing of your images. Users are encouraged to review their own Google AI Studio or Google Cloud privacy settings, as the use of a personal API key falls under the user's individual agreement with Google.

2.2 Biometric Data

What we access: We use your device's biometric capabilities (FaceID, TouchID, or Fingerprint).

Why: To provide secure, quick access to your financial data.

Data Handling: We never access, collect, or store your actual biometric data (fingerprints or facial maps). Authentication is handled entirely by your device's operating system, which simply tells our app "Success" or "Failure".

2.3 User Content (Financial Data) & Data Transit

Internal: We do not collect, sell, share, or store your personal data on external developer servers. Information you input—transaction history, budgets, and goals—resides locally on your device.

Transit: For AI-powered features to function (such as receipt analysis), data you explicitly select is transmitted securely to Google's Gemini API as described in Section 2.1.

Data Handling: We employ a Hybrid Encryption model to balance privacy and performance:

  • Sensitive Data (Encrypted): Private values such as transaction amounts, notes, receipts, and investment quantities are encrypted using AES-256 before being stored.
  • Metadata (Raw): To ensure the app remains fast and searchable, non-sensitive metadata such as transaction dates, categories, and types (e.g., "Expense") are stored in plain text.

This ensures your private financial details remain hidden while allowing for instant search and rapid filtering.
