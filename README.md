# WealthSnap — Your Financial Mirror

**Version:** v1.8.0  
**Last Updated:** February 3, 2026  
**Status:** Closed Testing (12-Tester Milestone)

---

**WealthSnap** is an offline-first financial mirror built for absolute privacy. It provides a high-resolution reflection of your assets and progress—kept securely on your device, exactly where it belongs.

Built by a developer who wanted a better way to see the "truth" in their numbers, WealthSnap combines **bank-grade security** with **high-precision math**, without the subscriptions, ads, or cloud-sync trade-offs found in modern apps.

---

## 🔭 Vision & Philosophy

- **Your Standing, Reflected:** WealthSnap is a reflection tool, not an advisor. It doesn't tell you what to do; it provides a high-precision mirror of your data so you can see your reality clearly.
- **Privacy as a Foundation:** We don't just "protect" your data—we don't even have it. Your financial life stays in your pocket, not on a server.
- **Accuracy Above All:** Every cent matters. WealthSnap uses an arbitrary-precision engine to ensure your totals reconcile perfectly over years of history.

---

## 🌍 What WealthSnap Does (In Plain Terms)

- **Track Everything:** Income, expenses, transfers, and asset growth in one unified view.
- **Investment Mirror:** Monitor your stocks, cost basis, and portfolio allocation without linking to a brokerage.
- **Smart Calendar:** See your "Safe-to-Spend" balance and visualize upcoming bills before they happen.
- **Local Insights:** Understand your savings rate, burn rate, and financial runway using your own historical trends.

---

## 🔐 Privacy & Security by Design

> Even the developer cannot see your data—because it never leaves your device.

- **100% Offline-First:** No servers, no accounts, and no tracking. Works perfectly in a tunnel or on a plane.
- **Hybrid Encryption:** Sensitive fields like notes and amounts are locked with **AES-256 encryption** using your device's secure hardware.
- **Total Ownership:** Your data is stored in a local SQLite database. You own the file, you own the backups, and you own the privacy.

---

## 🤖 Smart Tools (You Control the Key)

WealthSnap includes optional AI features designed with **Architectural Integrity**:

- **AI Receipt Scanning:** Quickly extract totals and dates from receipts using on-device machine learning.
- **Asset Price Fetching:** Keep your portfolio value updated using a "Double AI" engine that researches and validates market data.
- **Bring Your Own Key (BYOK):** To keep the app free and private, you use your own Google Gemini API key. You pay only for what you use, directly to the provider.

---

## 💡 Why WealthSnap is Free

WealthSnap was built as a passion project for personal use. Because of its efficient architecture, it doesn't cost anything to run:

1.  **No Servers, No Costs:** Since your data stays on your phone, there are no hosting bills to pay.
2.  **No Data to Sell:** There is no "catch" because there is no data collection.
3.  **Built for the Craft:** This app exists because the developer wanted a tool that didn't exist elsewhere—and decided to share it.
