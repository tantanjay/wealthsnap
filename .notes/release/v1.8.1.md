# WealthSnap v1.8.1 – Release Notes

**Release Date:** February 3, 2026  
**Build:** Version 1.8.1 (Maintenance Release)

---

## 🐛 Bug Fixes

- Fixed an issue where the **Investment Portfolio** screen would not immediately update after adding a new investment.
- **Smart Alert Notifications**: Fixed a delay where alerts relied solely on background checks. Now, anomaly detection runs immediately after saving a transaction for real-time alerts.
- **Safe-To-Spend Logic Overhaul**:
    - Now calculates **True Discretionary Income** ("Guilt-Free Money").
    - **Formula**: `(Current Cash + Future Income) - (Future Bills + Burn Rate) = Safe-to-Spend`.
    - **Future Income**: Automatically includes recurring Income & Transfer-In expected before period end.
    - **Survival Mode**: Protects you by deducting your "Life Burnrate" (average daily non-recurring spend from the last 3 months) for the remaining days of the period.
    - **Strict Transfers**: Identifying "Transfer Out" as an expense to ensure you don't overspend money meant for savings.

## 🔭 UX Enhancements

- Refined **Financial Runway** calculation to include Transfer In/Out transactions for a more accurate liquid balance representation.
- **New Smart Alert**: Added **Runway Drop Detection**! You will now receive a notification if your financial runway drops by 25% or more compared to the previous month, helping you catch lifestyle creep early.
- **UI Improvement**: Standardized header titles and navigation button layout across Home, Investment, and History screens for a cohesive visual experience.
- **Holdings Sorter**: Added new sorting options to the **Current Holdings** list!
    - Your sort preference is **automatically saved** for your next session.
- **Home Dashboard Improvements**:
    - **Monthly Investment Card**: Added a swipeable card to toggle between **Total Portfolio** and **Monthly Activity** (showing Monthly Invested, Realized/Unrealized P/L).
    - **Display Preferences**: You can now set your preferred default view for the Investment Card (Total vs. Month) via Home Settings.
- **Investment Management**: You can now **Edit** or **Delete** investments directly from the History screen.
    - **Smart Consistency**:
        - Updates to an investment automatically sync with its linked transaction.
        - Deleting an investment cascades to remove the corresponding transaction record.
        - Linked transactions in History are now locked to prevent accidental data mismatches.
- **Investment Customization**: Added a new **Settings Menu** to the Portfolio screen, allowing granular reordering of both the **Stats Cards** and **Dashboard Sections** to suit your personal workflow.
- **Allocation Insights**:
    - Added a **Type** filter to the Allocation Chart to visualize portfolio distribution by asset class (Stocks, Funds, Crypto, etc.).
    - Refined the **Stocks** view to strictly display equity holdings, while the **Sector** view continues to provide a comprehensive overview of all assets.
- Improved **Reminders List**: Items are now sorted by upcoming schedule, with visual indicators (✅) for active notifications.
- Integrated **Change Logs** into the Help Center, allowing users to view version history and release notes directly within the app.
- **Smart Advisor Refinement**: Suggestions are now smarter and strictly contextual, analyzing only the **Active Stocks** in your portfolio to provide relevant "Crash", "Dip", or "Balance" alerts.
- **Budgeting**: Enhanced Budget Modal with a cleaner UI, improved list view, and smoother interactions.
- Updated the **Developer Message** in the Profile section to share the personal story and commitment behind the project.
- Added **Vision, Philosophy & Goals** modal in the Profile screen to transparently share WealthSnap's mission and core values.

## 📝 Terms & Privacy Update

- Updated Terms & Privacy to clarify local-only design, AI usage, encryption, data deletion, and governing law.

## 🛠 Developer Updates

- **Project Refactoring**: Internal code cleanup and modularization of all screens and components for improved maintainability and scalability.
