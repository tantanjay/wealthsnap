# WealthSnap v1.8.1 – Release Notes

**Release Date:** February 3, 2026  
**Build:** Version 1.8.1 (Maintenance Release)

---

## 🐛 Bug Fixes

- Fixed an issue where the **Investment Portfolio** screen would not immediately update after adding a new investment.

## 🔭 UX Enhancements

- Added **Vision, Philosophy & Goals** modal in the Profile screen to transparently share WealthSnap's mission and core values.
- Integrated **Change Logs** into the Help Center, allowing users to view version history and release notes directly within the app.
- Updated the **Developer Message** in the Profile section to share the personal story and commitment behind the project.
- Improved **Reminders List**: Items are now sorted by upcoming schedule, with visual indicators (✅) for active notifications.
- Refined **Financial Runway** calculation to include Transfer In/Out transactions for a more accurate liquid balance representation.
- **Investment Management**: You can now **Edit** or **Delete** investments directly from the History screen.
- **Smart Consistency**:
    - Updates to an investment automatically sync with its linked transaction.
    - Deleting an investment cascades to remove the corresponding transaction record.
    - Linked transactions in History are now locked to prevent accidental data mismatches.
- **Visual Polish**: Revamped the **Investment History** list with a premium card design and cleaner action buttons.

## 📝 Terms & Privacy Update

- Clarified WealthSnap’s **local-only** design (no user accounts or server-side data).
- Specified **direct device-to-Gemini API** data transmission for AI features.
- Added details on **AES-256 hybrid encryption** and secure key storage on device.
- Reinforced **permanent data deletion** when the app is removed.
- Updated **governing law** to the Republic of the Philippines (Data Privacy Act of 2012).
