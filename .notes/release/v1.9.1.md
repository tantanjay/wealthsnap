# WealthSnap v1.9.1 – Release Notes

**Release Date:** February 13, 2026
**Build:** Version 1.9.1 (Feature Release)

---

## 🏥 Financial Health Card

- **New Home Screen Dashboard**: A dedicated card at the top of your Home Screen to track your key financial vitals.
- **Interactive Views**: Swipe between two views:
    - **Total Assets**: Your Net Worth snapshot (Investments + Liquid Cash Flow).
    - **Health Metrics**: Your Runway, Budget Health, and Top Investment Holding.
- **Smart Insights**:
    - **Runway Trend**: See how your financial runway has changed compared to last month (e.g., "+0.5 months vs last month").
    - **Spending Pacing**: Real-time "Pulse" check on your spending velocity compared to your 3-month average (e.g., "Spending 10% lower than usual").
