# WealthSnap v1.7.0 – Release Notes

**Release Date:** February 1, 2026  
**Build:** Version 1.7.0 (Feature Release)

## ✨ What's New

### 🧠 Smart Alerts: Budget Aware
- **Budget Integration:** Smart Alerts now check your budgets! If you exceed your set budget for a category, you will receive an immediate alert, even if you don't have historical spending data.
- **Precision Detection:** Alerts are now triggered by specific **Category Items** (e.g., "Water", "Groceries") rather than broad groups for maximum accuracy.

### 🎨 Modernized Record Menu
- **New Visual Design:** A sleek, grid-based layout with grouped sections for Transactions, Investments, AI Tools, and Debt.
- **Improved Navigation:** Horizontal scrolling categories allow for quick access to all record types without clutter.
- **Tablet Friendly:** Optimized layout constraints ensure the menu looks great on large screens and in landscape mode.

### 💸 Transfer Form & Tracking
- **Dedicated Transfer UI:** A brand new simplified form specifically for recording Transfers.
- **In/Out Support:** Easily toggle between Incoming (Money In) and Outgoing (Money Out) transfers.
- **History Integration:** Transfers are now distinctively marked in your History with dedicated icons and colors, and are correctly factored into your monthly Income/Expense summaries.

### 📈 Investment Recording
- **Stock Purchases & Sales:** New dedicated form to track Stock transactions, including Symbol, Quantity, Price, and Fees.
- **Dividend & Interest Tracking:** Easily record Dividend income and Interest payments directly within the investment flow.
- **Smart Totals:** Automatically calculates the total transaction value (Quantity * Price + Fees) as you type.
- **Quick Calculator:** Integrated calculator access for price and fee fields to handle complex calculations on the fly.
- **Portfolio Computation:** Your Investment Card now properly calculates:
    - **Total Portfolio Value:** Based on the latest known price from your transaction history.
    - **Unrealized P/L:** Tracks your paper gains/losses relative to your cost basis.
    - **Realized P/L:** Automatically sums up your Capital Gains and Losses from sold assets.
- **Profit/Loss Visibility:** Your history list now directly shows the realized Profit or Loss for every SELL transaction, complete with color-coded values for instant clarity.

### 📊 Investment Insights
- **Smart Insights:** A monitoring tool that continuously analyzes market data to surface relevant stats:
    - **Drop Detection:** Identifies significant market drops (>10%) relative to the 30-day peak.
    - **Dip Detection:** Flags short-term price pullbacks relative to recent trends.
    - **Dividend Alerts:** Highlights stocks with upcoming ex-dividend dates.
    - **Balance Check:** Highlights assets that are underweight in your portfolio.
- **Allocation Heatmap:** Visualize your portfolio diversification like a pro!
    - **Interactive Treemap:** View your holdings as a color-coded heatmap where size represents value and color represents performance (Green/Red).
    - **Dual Views:** Instantly toggle between **Stock** view (individual performance) and **Sector** view (industry exposure).
- **Holdings List:** A comprehensive view of all your assets featuring live market prices, total value, and daily changes.
- **AI Price Fetch:** 🤖 Bring your portfolio to life! Use the new **Fetch Menu** (Gemini icon) to update historical stock prices and dividend history using AI.
    - **Smart Estimation:** Leverages generative AI to estimate historical price data when live APIs are unavailable.
    - **Background Sync:** Updates happen silently in the background so you can keep using the app uninterrupted.
    - **Flexible Duration:** Choose to fetch prices for Today, 3 Days, 7 Days, or up to the last month.
- **Dividend Chart:** 📊 Visualize your income stream!
    - **Monthly Projections:** A new bar chart shows your projected dividend income for each month of the year.
    - **Real Data:** Powered by your actual holdings and historical dividend records.

### 🏠 Home Screen Enhancements
- **New "Monthly Net" Card:** Added a dedicated card to view your pure *Income vs. Expense* performance, excluding transfers. This gives you a clear picture of your actual savings for the month.
- **Clarified Balances:** Updated label terminology to "Money In" and "Money Out" on main balance cards to clearly indicate that transfers are included.
- **"How is this Calculated?":** Tap the new info icon (ℹ️) on any balance card to see exactly what goes into the formula (e.g., whether transfers are included or excluded).

### 🛍️ Smart Category Selection
- **Recent Categories:** The transaction form now intelligently suggests your most used categories from the last 30 days.
- **Quick Access:** Replaced the dropdown with a horizontal "Quick Select" bar for faster data entry.
- **Search Integration:** Easily search your full category list directly from the quick select bar.

### 📅 Smart History Calendar
- **Interactive Calendar View:** A powerful new way to view your history! Switch to calendar mode to see your monthly spending at a glance.
- **Visual Analytics:** Dates are color-coded with proportional bars showing Income, Expense, and Transfers for each day.
- **💰 Safe-to-Spend:** Know exactly what's available! Automatically calculates your balance minus upcoming recurring bills for the period.
- **🌡️ Guilt Filter:** Identify spending habits with ease. A heat map highlights discretionary spending intensity, while ignoring fixed recurring bills like rent.
- **👻 Ghost Forecast:** See the future! Upcoming dates display badges showing the total amount of expected recurring bills.
- **ℹ️ Interactive Guide:** Tap the info icon to learn about these new Smart Stats with helpful visual examples.
- **Investment Signals:** Visual BUY/SELL indicators on the calendar help you correlate market moves with your personal finance timeline.

### ⚡ Performance & Stability
- **Concurrency Safety:** Hardened the transaction loading process so you can safely add new items even while the app is still decrypting historical data in the background.
- **Data Integrity:** Fixed a potential race condition where optimistic updates (immediate UI feedback) could be overwritten by finishing background loads.
- **Investment Optimization:** Refactored the investment calculation engine to use O(N) grouping, significantly speeding up portfolio loading times for users with large transaction histories.
- **Database:** Implemented automated migrations for the price history table to ensure data consistency.

## 🐛 Bug Fixes

### 🔧 Core Logic
- **Anomaly Calculation:** Fixed a critical issue where historical averages were calculated per transaction instead of per month. This ensures that spending spikes are identified based on accurate monthly trends.
- **Infinite Loop Fix:** Resolved a critical bug where setting a 'Semi-Weekly' reminder could cause the app to hang indefinitely due to a date calculation error.
