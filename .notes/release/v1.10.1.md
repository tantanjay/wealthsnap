# WealthSnap v1.10.1 – Release Notes

**Release Date:** February 17, 2026
**Build:** Version 1.10.1 (Maintenance Release)

---

## 💾 Backup & Restore

- **Debt Backup Support**: Your Debt records are now fully included in backups.
    - **Secure Restore**: Debts are encrypted and safely restored along with your other financial data.
    - **Seamless Migration**: Moving to a new device? Your loans, credit cards, and other liabilities will transfer over perfectly.

## 💰 Debt Management

- **Improved Fee Handling**: When adding a debt that was paid directly to a vendor (Option 2), you can now record any associated fees as an expense.
    - **Accurate Tracking**: Ensures your debt amount is correct while still capturing the cost of borrowing (fees) in your expenses.

## 📈 Refined Wealth Growth Metrics

- **Verified Investment Projections**: Your "Current Path" now strictly counts investments backed by verified Cash Flow (transaction records), ensuring accuracy over manual portfolio updates.
- **Conservative Capacity**: "Potential Investment" calculations now exclude partial-month income, preventing inflated surplus estimates when income arrives before expenses are paid.
- **Improved Forecasting**: Better handling for scenarios where current savings don't lead to financial freedom, with clearer paths showing how investing changes the outcome from "Forever" to "Possible".
- **Real Net Flow**: Cash Flow calculations now include transfers. Investment contributions are deducted from "Cash Savings" to avoid double-counting, ensuring your "Wealth Growth" stats are purely additive to your runway.
