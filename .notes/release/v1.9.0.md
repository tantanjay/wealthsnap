# WealthSnap v1.9.0 – Release Notes

**Release Date:** February 12, 2026
**Build:** Version 1.9.0 (Feature Release)

---

## 📈 Insights

- **Interactive Trends**: The Savings Rate Trend chart is now fully interactive!
    - **Tap-to-Inspect**: Tap any point on the chart to instantly see a detailed popup with your precise Savings Rate, Income, and Expense for that month.
- **Income & Comparison Details**: Press and hold on the Income Analysis or Spending Comparison bars to view the exact actual and projected amounts.

---

## 🌍 Native Currency Support

- **Invest in Any Currency**: Record investments in their native currency (e.g., USD, EUR, JPY) - perfect for international stocks or crypto.
- **Smart Conversion & Display**: 
    - **Auto-Fetch Rates**: Automatically fetches exchange rates via **Frankfurter API**.
    - **Portfolio Valuation**: Converts historical prices to your profile currency for accurate total net worth calculation.
    - **Native Context**: In your history view, prices are displayed in their *original* currency (e.g., seeing $150.00 for Apple stock) while the value is tracked in your home currency.

---

## ⚡ Performance

- **Optimized Data Sync**: New background service caches exchange rates and market data for faster loading and reduced API usage.

---

## 🚀 Crypto Support

- **Crypto Investment**: Added "Crypto" as a selectable investment type.

---

## 🐛 Bug Fixes

- **Monthly Unrealized P/L**: Fixed calculation logic where sold investments were incorrectly contributing to monthly unrealized gains/losses. Now correctly caps based on current holdings.
