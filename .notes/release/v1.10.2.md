# WealthSnap v1.10.2 – Release Notes

**Release Date:** March 04, 2026
**Build:** Version 1.10.2 (Maintenance Release)

---

## 💸 Transaction Recording

- **Smart Category Selection**: Improved the category picking experience during transaction entry.
    - **Smart Search Scrolling**: Selecting a category via the search modal now automatically scrolls the horizontal list to bring your selection into focus.
    - **Stabilized View**: The scroll logic ensures that the "Search" button remains visible, even when new categories are dynamically added to the front of your recent list.
    - **Context-Aware Scrolling**: Automated scrolling is now only triggered when using the search interface, preventing unwanted horizontal jumps during direct taps on the category list.

## 🔁 Recurring Transactions & Reminders

- **Bi-Annual Frequency**: Added support for **Bi-Annual** (every 6 months) recurrence for both transactions and reminders.
    - **Long-term Planning**: Perfect for tracking semi-annual bills, insurance premiums, or scheduled maintenance tasks.
    - **Automated Catch-up**: The recurrence engine correctly handles "catching up" on missed bi-annual entries if the app hasn't been opened for an extended period.
    - **Smart Notifications**: Reminders now support the bi-annual pattern, ensuring you're alerted exactly 6 months from your start date.

## 💖 Community

- **Thanks to our Supporters**: Added a new Supporter Screen honoring the legendary individuals who back WealthSnap. This features custom animated styles and a full roster list.

## 🛠️ Maintenance & Refinement

- **Platform Compatibility**: Internal updates to ensure smooth operation across recent Android and iOS platform variations.
- **Dependency Security**: Completed a security audit and updated core dependencies to their latest stable versions.
