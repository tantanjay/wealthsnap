# WealthSnap v1.14.0 – Release Notes

**Release Date:** July 19, 2026
**Build:** Version 1.14.0 (Feature & UI Update)

---

## 📊 Savings Trend: Rate, Saved & Cash Flow
The Savings Trend chart on Insights now shows more than just a percentage.

- **Three Views**: Switch between **Rate** (the % of income you kept), **Saved** (the raw amount kept, including money moved into investments), and **Cash Flow** (what's actually left in cash after everything, including investments and other transfers out).
- **Consistent Stats**: Streak, Positive/Negative counts, Average, Current, and Best/Worst Month all recalculate for whichever view you're on.

---

## 🎛️ Streamlined Time Range Selector
The 6M / 1Y / 3Y / ALL range toggle on Insights charts has been redesigned for small screens.

- **Compact Dropdown**: The four-button row is now a single dropdown pill showing the active range — tap it to pick from a clean list instead of squeezing four buttons into the header.
- **Applied Everywhere**: Rolled out consistently across the Income Trend, Spending Comparison, Savings Rate Trend, and Category Trend charts.
- **Uniform Chart Sizing**: All four charts now share the same container height and spacing, so switching between them feels consistent instead of each one jumping to a different size.

---

## 💾 Your Selections Now Stick
Chart tabs, time ranges, and toggles across the app now remember what you last picked, instead of resetting every time you leave the screen.

- **Insights**: Income Trend, Spending Comparison, and Savings Trend all remember their tab and time range (6M/1Y/3Y/ALL); Expense Analysis remembers Group vs. Item; Monthly Pulse remembers its 3M/6M/12M period.
- **Investments**: The Dividend chart remembers Actual/Calendar/Proj., the Allocation chart remembers Stocks/Sector/Type, and the Smart Advisor priority filter is remembered too.
- **Debt Strategy**: The Avalanche/Snowball payoff strategy toggle now persists.
- **Home**: Swiping a card (Financial Health, Cash Flow, Investments, Debts) to a different view now saves that choice immediately - no extra step needed. Removed the old Home Settings screen that required manually picking and saving a default view for each card, since swiping now does that automatically. The Recent/Top Expenses tab on the Home screen also remembers your last pick.

---

## ⚙️ Floating Quick Actions Gear
The Eye and Settings buttons on Home, Investments, Insights, and History can now break free of the header.

- **Drag to Detach**: Long-press and drag either button out of the header and both collapse into a single floating gear bubble, Assistive Touch–style.
- **Quick Actions Menu**: Tap the bubble for Hide/Show Balances and Screen Settings (List/Calendar toggle on History).
- **Drag to Re-Dock**: Drag the bubble back to the top-right to restore the normal header buttons; drop it anywhere else and it snaps to the nearest edge.
- **Reveal for Screenshot**: New quick action to temporarily allow a screenshot with real numbers visible (asks for confirmation first) — screenshot protection automatically resumes a few seconds later, and nothing about the reveal is saved.

---

## 🛠️ Stability & Layout Fixes
- **Fixed Dividend Calendar tab sizing glitch**: Switching to the Dividend Calendar tab and back could leave a large blank gap or stale chart height behind, caused by the tab content reusing the same layout measurement across drastically different content shapes. Each tab now remounts cleanly and re-measures its own height.
- **Fixed Dividend Calendar excess height in portrait**: The month cells were forced into perfect squares, which made them far taller than their content needed in portrait's 3-column layout. Cells now size to their content instead, shrinking the 4-row grid from ~440px down to ~250px.

---

## 🤖 AI Engine Upgrade
The AI features behind receipt scanning and historical price/dividend fetching (BYOK Gemini) have been upgraded under the hood.

- **Upgraded to Gemini 3.5 Flash**: The default model used for receipt analysis and historical price/dividend lookups is now Gemini 3.5 Flash, replacing 2.5 Flash — faster responses with stronger reasoning for the same "Bring Your Own Key" setup.
- **Smarter reasoning depth per task**: Straightforward jobs like extracting a receipt or reformatting research into JSON now run with lighter reasoning for speed, while the research passes that search the web for historical prices and dividends run with deeper reasoning for accuracy.
- **Fixed AI usage cost estimates**: The Usage Log in Profile settings now reads Gemini's actual token counts instead of estimating from response length, so cost totals correctly include reasoning ("thinking") tokens that were previously left out of the estimate.

---

## 📦 Dependency Updates
- **Updated Expo SDK 55 packages**: Bumped Expo and its related packages (background-task, clipboard, constants, dev-client, document-picker, file-system, image-manipulator, image-picker, linear-gradient, local-authentication, navigation-bar, notifications, screen-capture, secure-store, sharing, sqlite, store-review, system-ui, task-manager, updates) to their latest patch versions for stability and performance.
