# WealthSnap v1.12.0 – Release Notes

**Release Date:** May 09, 2026
**Build:** Version 1.12.0 (Features and Bugs Fixes Update)

---

## 📈 New & Improved Dividend Dashboard
We've completely redesigned the dividend section to make it easier for you to track your passive income and see exactly where your money is coming from.

- **New Tabbed Layout**: Easily switch between your **Actual** earnings, a **Calendar** of payouts, and your **Projected** income—all in a design that matches your investment charts.
- **Interactive Breakdowns**: Just tap any bar in your chart to see exactly which stocks or assets paid you that month.
- **Automatic Tracking**: The app now automatically captures dividends you log as direct income (like MP2), ensuring your charts are always accurate and complete.

---

## 🧠 Smart Advisor Enhancements
We've refined the logic and interface of our Smart Advisor for a smoother experience.

- **Improved Consistency**: Fixed an issue with inconsistent "Balance" suggestions. Alerts are now intelligently merged and prioritized.
- **Better Navigation**: Resolved pagination and scrolling issues in the carousel.
- **Enhanced Guidance**: Added an "Alert Hierarchy" section in the info modal to explain how suggestions are prioritized.

---

## 🛠️ Maintenance & Stability
- **Updated Dependencies**: We've updated all underlying app components to their latest versions to ensure the app stays fast, secure, and compatible with the latest mobile updates.
