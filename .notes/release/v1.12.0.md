# WealthSnap v1.12.0 – Release Notes

**Release Date:** May 09, 2026
**Build:** Version 1.12.0 (Feature and Bug Fix Updates)

---

## 📅 Historical Monthly Insights
You can now travel back in time! We've added a powerful new month and year selector to the Financial Insights screen.

- **Full Historical Browsing**: Tap the new navigation arrows at the top of the Insights screen to view detailed analytics for any previous month and year.
- **Dynamic Context**: All charts, overview cards, and trend analyses now instantly react to your selected date, giving you a crystal-clear picture of your past financial performance.
- **Intelligent Projections**: The app now intelligently hides "projected" data when you're looking at historical months, ensuring you only see the real, finalized numbers for your past history.
- **"Back to Today" Shortcut**: Easily jump back to the current month with a single tap.

---

## 📈 New & Improved Dividend Dashboard
We've completely redesigned the dividend section to make it easier for you to track your passive income and see exactly where your money is coming from.

- **New Tabbed Layout**: Easily switch between your **Actual** earnings, a **Calendar** of payouts, and your **Projected** income—all in a design that matches your investment charts.
- **Interactive Breakdowns**: Just tap any bar in your chart to see exactly which stocks or assets paid you that month.
- **Automatic Tracking**: The app now automatically captures dividends you log as direct income (like MP2), ensuring your charts are always accurate and complete.

---

## 🧠 Smart Advisor Enhancements
We've refined the logic and interface of our Smart Advisor for a smoother experience.

- **Improved Consistency**: Fixed an issue with inconsistent "Balance" suggestions. Alerts are now intelligently merged and prioritized.
- **Better Navigation**: Resolved pagination and scrolling issues in the carousel.
- **Enhanced Guidance**: Added an "Alert Hierarchy" section in the info modal to explain how suggestions are prioritized.

---

## 📊 Insight & Analysis Upgrades
We've improved the Spending Comparison and Trend analysis tools to give you more flexibility.

- **Dynamic Comparison Toggle**: The Comparison chart now includes a **Trend** toggle, allowing you to switch between a month-over-month comparison and a historical bar graph.
- **Yearly Data Browsing**: When viewing the **1Y** trend in either Income or Expenses, you can now browse through historical years using the new year selector chevrons.
- **Projected Spending**: The current month now intelligently shows a projected spending bar based on your current pace, helping you stay on track before the month ends.

---

## 🛠️ Maintenance & Stability
- **Updated Dependencies**: We've updated all underlying app components to their latest versions to ensure the app stays fast, secure, and compatible with the latest mobile updates.
