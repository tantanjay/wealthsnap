# WealthSnap v1.10.0 – Release Notes

**Release Date:** February 14, 2026
**Build:** Version 1.10.0 (Feature Release)

---

## 🏥 Financial Health Card

- **New Home Screen Dashboard**: A dedicated card at the top of your Home Screen to track your key financial vitals.
    - **Interactive Views**: Swipe between specialized views for Health Metrics, Net Worth, and Total Assets.
- **Financial Health Details**: A comprehensive deep-dive into your financial trajectory.
    - **Financial State Analysis**: Tracks your "Stability Runway" (months of survival without income) and projects a specific "Liquidity Date".
    - **Spending & Cash Flow**: Monitors your "Net Flow" and "Spending Trend" baseline to calculate the real-world **Impact on Self-Sustain** (months added/removed from your Self-Sustain date per year).
    - **Debt Pressure**: Identifies your "Mandatory Payment" lock and "Interest Cost" drag. Shows how much **Debt Delays Self-Sustain** in years.
    - **Wealth Growth**: Visualizes "Self-Sustain Acceleration" from investments
- **Advanced Net Worth Tracking**:
    - **Conservative Projections**: Net Worth accounts for current principal plus projected future interest for a "True Cost" view.
    - **Liabilities Breakdown**: Clear visibility into mandatory obligations vs. discretionary cash flow.

---

## 💸 Debt & Loan Management

- **Comprehensive Debt Form**: Easily track all your liabilities including Loans, Credit Cards, Mortgages, and IOUs.
- **Detailed Payment Breakdown**: View a complete amortization schedule table showing Principal, Interest, and Remaining Balance for every month of your loan term.
- **Smart Calculations**:
    - **Auto-Calculate Min Payment**: Automatically computes monthly payments based on interest rate, term, and interest type (Fixed, Variable, Flat, None).
    - **True Cost Metrics**: Your **Runway** and **Safe-to-Spend** now account for mandatory Debt Obligations (Min Payments), giving you a more conservative and realistic view of your financial health.
    - **Accurate Savings Rate**: Your "Savings Rate" trend now correctly factors in debt repayments as expenses (without double counting), giving you a true picture of your net saving capacity after all obligations.
    - **Interest Types**: Support for various interest calculation methods to match real-world scenarios.
- **Smart Transaction Strategy**: WealthSnap now asks "Did this money enter your account?" after saving a debt. This helps you correctly handle scenarios like direct vendor payments (e.g., renovations) without inflating your cash balance.
- **Improved Debt Card**:
    - **Monthly vs Total Views**: Swipe between lifetime debt totals and current month's activity.
    - **Monthly Obligations**: See a clear breakdown of your total required minimum payments vs what you've actually paid this month.
    - **Smart Tracking**: "Repaid" tracks principal reduction via transfers, while "Interest" tracks expense cost separately.
- **Dedicated Debt Screen**: 
    - **Active Debts**: View all your active loans and liabilities in one place.
    - **One-Tap Payment**: Quickly record payments with a single tap.
    - **Smart Split**: Automatically splits your payment into Principal (Transfer) and Interest (Expense) based on your loan terms.
    - **Linked Transactions**: Deleting the principal payment automatically removes the associated interest record to keep your history clean.
