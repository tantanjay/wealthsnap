# WealthSnap v1.8.2 – Release Notes

**Release Date:** February 6, 2026
**Build:** Version 1.8.2 (Maintenance Release)

---

## 📈 Investments

- **Native Currency Support**: You can now record investments in their native currency (e.g., USD for US stocks) even if your profile is in a different currency.
- **Improved UI**: New eye-catching currency toggle in the investment form.

## 🛡️ Data Safety

- **Backup Reminder**: Added a smart reminder to prompt you for a backup if you haven't done so in 7 days.
- **Snooze Option**: Added a "Remind me later" option to defer the backup prompt.

## 💬 Community

- **App Review**: Added an option to rate WealthSnap directly from the app. Your feedback helps us improve!
