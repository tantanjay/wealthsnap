# WealthSnap v1.8.2 – Release Notes

**Release Date:** February 6, 2026
**Build:** Version 1.8.2 (Maintenance Release)

---

## 📈 Investments

- **Native Currency Support**: You can now record investments in their native currency (e.g., USD for US stocks) even if your profile is in a different currency.
- **Linked Transaction Currency**: The History Screen now correctly displays the native currency of an investment for its linked transactions (e.g., BUY/SELL actions).
- **Asset Filtering Fix**: Resolved an issue where the asset selector in the Investment Form showed all assets instead of filtering by the selected type (Stocks vs. Funds).

## 📊 Monthly Pulse

- **Tap-to-Inspect**: Tap and drag across the chart to see precise values for your actual spending, projected totals, and historical averages.

## 🛡️ Data Safety

- **Backup Reminder**: Added a smart reminder to prompt you for a backup if you haven't done so in 7 days.
- **Snooze Option**: Added a "Remind me later" option to defer the backup prompt.

## 💬 Stability

- **Android Crash Fix**: Upgraded the dropdown selection component to resolve a critical native crash affecting specific Android devices.

## 💬 Community

- **App Review**: Added an option to rate WealthSnap directly from the app. Your feedback helps us improve!

## 🔧 Under the Hood

- **Chart Library Upgrade**: Replaced the legacy `react-native-chart-kit` with a modern, actively maintained charting engine (`react-native-gifted-charts`) to ensure long-term stability and smoother interactions.
