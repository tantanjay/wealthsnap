# WealthSnap v1.13.0 – Release Notes

**Release Date:** July 5, 2026
**Build:** Version 1.13.0 (Bug Fix & Accuracy Update)

---

## 🎯 Debt Tracker Accuracy Fixes
A deep audit of the debt tracking math turned up several real accuracy bugs, especially around money you've **lent out** (Receivable debts). All fixed:

- **Fixed cash direction on debt payments**: Recording a payment on a Receivable debt ("money owed to me") no longer incorrectly deducts from your cash balance. Repayments now correctly add to your cash, and interest earned is now recorded as Income instead of an Expense.
- **Fixed Home Screen debt totals**: Repayments on Receivable debts weren't reducing your "Total Debt" and "Repaid" figures on the Home screen — they now do.
- **Fixed double-counted fees**: Creating a new debt with "Yes, I received the cash" and a processing fee no longer deducts the fee from your cash balance twice.
- **Fixed Debt-Free Date for Flat-interest loans**: The Debt Strategy screen's payoff simulation now correctly accounts for Flat-interest loans instead of treating every debt as a reducing-balance loan, which previously understated total interest and gave an overly optimistic debt-free date.
- **Fixed Savings Rate miscount**: Lending money out for the first time was being counted the same as an ongoing debt repayment, artificially tanking your Savings Rate for that month.

---

## 📈 Investment & Dividend Fixes

- **Fixed dividend currency mismatch**: Dividend Yield %, Projected Dividends, and the Dividend Calendar now correctly convert foreign-currency dividends (e.g. USD stocks) into your profile currency, instead of comparing raw foreign amounts against profile-currency prices. Previously, this could understate these figures by 50x or more for foreign holdings.

---

## 📅 Financial Insights: Historical Browsing Fixed for Real
Building on last release's month/year picker, several trend charts were silently ignoring your selected month and always showing data relative to today. Now fixed:

- **Spending Comparison** ("Avg 3M/6M/1Y" bars and Trend tab), **Income Trend**, **Savings Rate**, and **Monthly Pulse's** average comparison line all now correctly shift to match whichever month you're browsing, instead of quietly comparing your selected month against today's rolling averages.
- The in-chart year selector (used in the "1Y" trend view) now stays in sync with the month picker at the top of the screen.

---

## 💪 Financial Health Screen Fixes

- **Fixed Debt Pressure & Runway for lenders**: Money owed *to* you (Receivable debts) was being counted as your own liability — inflating your Financial Runway's burn rate, Debt Pressure's interest cost and total liability, and incorrectly blocking the "No active debt" message for anyone with zero real debt of their own.
- **Fixed Financial Independence projection**: "Years to Freedom" no longer defaults to a flat 500 years for users with an existing portfolio but no active monthly contribution — it now correctly accounts for compound growth on existing capital.
- **Improved Runway Change accuracy**: The month-over-month Runway trend now better estimates whether a since-paid-off debt was still active as of last month.

---

## 🛠️ Stability & Layout Fixes
- **Fixed clipped/broken scrolling**: The Dividend Calendar tab was clipping its 12-month grid inside a fixed-height box, and the Home, Profile, Investment, and Insights screens each nested a second scroll view inside their own outer scroll container — causing unreliable scrolling on some devices. The Home Screen's dashboard reorder menu had the same issue. All screens now scroll smoothly and show full content.
- **Updated Dependencies**: Patched known security vulnerabilities and updated underlying app components to their latest compatible versions, keeping the app secure and up to date.
