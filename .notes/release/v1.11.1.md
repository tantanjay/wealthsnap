# WealthSnap v1.11.1 – Release Notes

**Release Date:** March 11, 2026
**Build:** Version 1.11.1 (OTA Infrastructure)

---

## ⚡ Over-the-Air (OTA) Updates Enabled
This release enables **EAS Update** infrastructure, allowing us to deliver bug fixes and minor improvements directly to your app without requiring a new download from the Play Store.

- **EAS Update Integration**: Configured the app to support seamless over-the-air updates.
- **Improved Patching**: Enabled `bsdiff` patch support for smaller, faster update downloads.
- **Stability**: Enhanced update handling logic to ensure reliability during background synchronization.

## 🛠️ Deprecation Removal & Maintenance
- **Background Fetch API Migration**: Upgraded and refactored our background synchronization services from the deprecated `expo-background-fetch` to the new `expo-background-task` API to ensure continued stability and future compatibility.

## 🎨 UI/UX Improvements
- **Automatic Theme Matching**: The app now natively respects your device's system theme (Dark/Light mode)! The in-app "System" appearance option now perfectly syncs with your phone's OS preferences.
