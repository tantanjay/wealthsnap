# WealthSnap v1.7.0 – Release Notes

**Release Date:** January 29, 2026  
**Build:** Version 1.7.0 (Feature Release)

## ✨ What's New

### 🧠 Smart Alerts: Budget Aware
- **Budget Integration:** Smart Alerts now check your budgets! If you exceed your set budget for a category, you will receive an immediate alert, even if you don't have historical spending data.
- **Precision Detection:** Alerts are now triggered by specific **Category Items** (e.g., "Water", "Groceries") rather than broad groups for maximum accuracy.

### 🎨 Modernized Record Menu
- **New Visual Design:** A sleek, grid-based layout with grouped sections for Transactions, Investments, AI Tools, and Debt.
- **Improved Navigation:** Horizontal scrolling categories allow for quick access to all record types without clutter.
- **Tablet Friendly:** Optimized layout constraints ensure the menu looks great on large screens and in landscape mode.

### 💸 Transfer Form & Tracking
- **Dedicated Transfer UI:** A brand new simplified form specifically for recording Transfers.
- **In/Out Support:** Easily toggle between Incoming (Money In) and Outgoing (Money Out) transfers.
- **History Integration:** Transfers are now distinctively marked in your History with dedicated icons and colors, and are correctly factored into your monthly Income/Expense summaries.

### 🏠 Home Screen Enhancements
- **New "Monthly Net" Card:** Added a dedicated card to view your pure *Income vs. Expense* performance, excluding transfers. This gives you a clear picture of your actual savings for the month.
- **Clarified Balances:** Updated label terminology to "Money In" and "Money Out" on main balance cards to clearly indicate that transfers are included.
- **"How is this Calculated?":** Tap the new info icon (ℹ️) on any balance card to see exactly what goes into the formula (e.g., whether transfers are included or excluded).

### ⚡ Performance & Stability
- **Concurrency Safety:** Hardened the transaction loading process so you can safely add new items even while the app is still decrypting historical data in the background.
- **Data Integrity:** Fixed a potential race condition where optimistic updates (immediate UI feedback) could be overwritten by finishing background loads.

## 🐛 Bug Fixes

### 🔧 Core Logic
- **Anomaly Calculation:** Fixed a critical issue where historical averages were calculated per transaction instead of per month. This ensures that spending spikes are identified based on accurate monthly trends.
