# Financial Insights — Cards & Charts Overview

This document explains **what each card and chart shows** and **the user value behind it**.  
Focus: insights, not formulas.

---

## 🧾 Overview Cards (Quick Financial Snapshot)

These cards answer **“Am I financially okay right now?”** at a glance.

### 1. Financial Runway
**Shows:**  
How many months your current balance can support your lifestyle.

**Why it matters:**  
- Measures financial safety
- Highlights risk early

**User insight:**  
> “I can sustain my current spending for _X_ months.”

---

### 2. Budget Health
**Shows:**  
How much of your monthly budgets you’ve used (percentage).

**Why it matters:**  
- Early warning before overspending
- Easier to understand than raw numbers

**User insight:**  
> “My spending is healthy / getting risky.”

---

### 3. Net Cash Flow
**Shows:**  
Income minus expenses for the current month.

**Why it matters:**  
- Indicates progress or regression
- Simple profitability signal

**User insight:**  
> “I’m moving forward / falling behind this month.”

---

### 4. Savings Rate
**Shows:**  
Percentage of income saved this month.

**Why it matters:**  
- Standard wealth metric
- Scales across any income level

**User insight:**  
> “How efficiently am I saving what I earn?”

---

### 5. Total Income
**Shows:**  
All income received this month.

**Why it matters:**  
- Baseline for all other insights
- Useful for variable income earners

**User insight:**  
> “This is how much I earned.”

---

### 6. Total Expense
**Shows:**  
All spending recorded this month.

**Why it matters:**  
- Clear cost awareness
- No category noise

**User insight:**  
> “This is my true monthly cost.”

---

### 7. Burn Rate
**Shows:**  
Average monthly spending based on recent history.

**Why it matters:**  
- More realistic than a single month
- Powers runway and projections

**User insight:**  
> “This is what my lifestyle actually costs.”

---

### 8. Daily Average
**Shows:**  
Average amount spent per day this month.

**Why it matters:**  
- Helps pace spending mid-month
- Easy mental model

**User insight:**  
> “I spend about _X_ per day.”

---

### 9. Annualized Expense
**Shows:**  
Estimated yearly spending based on burn rate.

**Why it matters:**  
- Connects daily habits to long-term impact
- Helps plan income needs

**User insight:**  
> “This lifestyle costs me about _X_ per year.”

---

### 10. Top Category
**Shows:**  
Highest spending category this month.

**Why it matters:**  
- Identifies biggest money drain
- Highlights optimization opportunities

**User insight:**  
> “Most of my money goes here.”

---

## 📈 Charts (Trends & Behavioral Insights)

Charts answer **“Where am I heading?”**, not just where I am.

---

## Savings Rate Trend
**Shows:**  
Monthly savings performance over time.

**Why it matters:**  
- Reveals consistency
- Highlights improvement or decline

**Key insights:**
- Average savings rate
- Positive vs negative months
- Current saving or overspending streak

**User insight:**  
> “Am I becoming a better saver?”

---

## Cumulative Spending Chart
**Shows:**  
How fast you’re spending this month vs your historical average.

**Why it matters:**  
- Detects overspending early
- Predicts month-end outcome

**Includes:**
- Actual spending (solid line)
- Projected spending (dotted line)
- Pacing insight (above / below average)

**User insight:**  
> “Am I spending faster than usual?”

---

## Monthly Comparison Chart
**Shows:**  
This month compared to last month and historical averages.

**Why it matters:**  
- Adds context to partial-month data
- Prevents overreaction

**Comparison bars:**
- This month (projected)
- Last month
- 3-month average
- 6-month average
- 1-year average

**User insight:**  
> “Is this month actually bad or just normal?”

---

## Income Analysis
**Shows:**  
Income stability and growth trends.

**Why it matters:**  
- Highlights volatility
- Useful for freelancers and variable income users

**Insights provided:**
- Growth or decline percentage
- Projection vs historical average

**User insight:**  
> “Is my income improving or unstable?”

---

## Expense Analysis
**Shows:**  
Spending breakdown by category with budget awareness.

**Why it matters:**  
- Surfaces actionable problem areas
- Prioritizes over-budget categories

**Smart behavior:**
- Over-budget categories ranked first
- Budget progress shown visually

**User insight:**  
> “What should I fix first?”

---

## 🚨 Smart Alerts
**Shows:**  
Automatically detected unusual spending behavior.

**Why it matters:**  
- Catches anomalies users may miss
- Reduces mental load

**Examples:**
- Spending spike alerts
- Category anomaly warnings

**User insight:**  
> “Something looks off — I should review this.”

---

## 🧠 Overall Value

Financial Insights turn **raw transactions** into **clear, actionable understanding** by:
- Showing direction, not just totals
- Highlighting risk early
- Helping users take control, not just track
