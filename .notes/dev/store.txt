# WealthSnap – Private Financial OS

Take control of your money with clarity, precision, and complete privacy.

WealthSnap is a powerful, offline-first personal finance app that helps you understand not just where your money goes — but how your decisions affect your survival, stability, and long-term freedom.

No cloud dependency.  
No forced subscriptions.  
No data harvesting.  

Just intelligent financial control — fully on your device.

---

## 🚀 WHY WEALTHSNAP IS DIFFERENT

Most finance apps track spending.  
WealthSnap models your financial life.

It calculates:

- 💰 **Safe-to-Spend** (true discretionary income)
- 🏃 **Financial Runway** (months you can survive without income)
- 📅 **Freedom / Liquidity Date**
- 📉 **Debt Drag & Interest Cost**
- 📈 **Investment Acceleration**
- 📊 **Savings Rate & Spending Trends**

You don’t just see numbers — you see trajectory.

---

## 🔐 100% PRIVATE & LOCAL

- AES-256 encrypted storage

## 💳 DEBT & LOAN MANAGEMENT

Track loans, credit cards, mortgages, and IOUs with:

- Full amortization schedules
- Automatic minimum payment calculation
- Principal vs interest breakdown
- Debt-aware Safe-to-Spend calculations
- Interest cost measured as “freedom delay”

Your obligations are fully integrated into your financial health.
