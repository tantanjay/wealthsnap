- See your balance and monthly spending at a glance

*Perfect if you just want to track your money without diving into complex insights.*

## Explorer
**Unlock full insights and financial intelligence.**

- Access Safe-to-Spend, Runway, Financial Health, and Debt Analytics  
- Explore advanced metrics and projections  
- Discover patterns, plan your finances, and optimize your future  

*Perfect if you want a deeper, strategic view of your money.*

> **Icon suggestion:** Growth chart or telescope
